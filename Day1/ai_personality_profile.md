## 7. What You Need to Improve to Become Elite

### Define session goals before starting
You often explore freely — great for discovery, bad for shipping. Start with "what's my output?" before opening a chat.

### Build reusable prompt systems
Move from ad-hoc prompts to a personal prompt library. Templates, system prompts, and structured formats for repeat tasks.

### Integrate AI into your tools stack
Stop treating AI as a standalone. Pipe it into your workflow — code editors, notes, automation. That's where compounding happens.

### Ship before perfect
Your research instinct is an asset and a blocker. Set a "good enough to ship" threshold and publish the work — feedback is your next research round.
