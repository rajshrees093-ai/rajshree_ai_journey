## Key Learnings

### What I Learned

* ATS systems heavily depend on keywords and standardized formatting.
* Proper section headings improve resume parsing accuracy.
* Action verbs make project and experience descriptions stronger.
* Categorized skills improve readability for recruiters.
* AI can quickly identify weaknesses that are often overlooked manually.
* Small changes in wording can significantly increase ATS scores.

### Biggest Takeaway

Claude transformed my resume from an ATS score of **48/100** to **89/100**, achieving a **41-point improvement (85% gain)**. This exercise demonstrated how AI can be used as a powerful career development tool to improve resume quality, ATS compatibility, and recruiter appeal within minutes.
